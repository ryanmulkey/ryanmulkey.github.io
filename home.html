About Me

Graduate Mechanical Engineering Student

I am a senior mechanical engineering student at Louisiana Tech University, and am enrolled in a Master's degree in mechanical engineering.
Currently, I serve as President of Louisiana Tech’s Pi Tau Sigma chapter. Previously, I held the role of President for both the Louisiana Tech American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME) student chapter and the Louisiana Tech Orthodox Christian Fellowship (OCF), where I helped foster both technical engagement and community development.
In 2021, I was honored to be a co-recipient of the Congressional App Challenge Award, recognizing innovation in software development at the national level.
